Wheel For Change

Wheel for change was an investment platform for Social Entrepreneurs where Potential investors could find and invest in projects. The Client required a digital platform in which Social entrepreneurs could create their profiles for their projects and could be found by investors.

Client

Ashraful Hasan

My Role

Product Designer

Duration

3 months

Challenges

Social Entrepreneurs had to input in a lot of information through a form and forms can be long, confusing, and time-consuming. This could affect the experience of the user and drive him away from the platform. The challenge here is to design a form that would be easy to understand and fill.

User Research

We interviewed 6 people to Understanding Social Entrepreneurs and their problems and based on that determine a set of features to solve their problems

Key Outcomes

  • Manage my team and distribute tasks
  • Meet other entrepreneurs who believe in the same cause as I do
  • Connect with investors with the same social values as myself
  • Spend less time doing paperwork

User Flows

Tasks

With the help of user flows we identified various tasks a social entrprenuer would perform and also differentiated which part of the journey will be free and which one should be paid.
