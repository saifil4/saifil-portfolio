Wheel For Change

An Investment Platform for Social Entrepreneurs

About Project

Wheel for change was an platform for Social Entrepreneurs where Potential investors could find and invest in projects. The Client required a digital platform in which Social entrepreneurs could create their profiles for their projects and could be found by investors.

Client

Ashraful Hasan

My Role

Product Designer

Duration

3 months

Challenges

Social Entrepreneurs had to input in a lot of information through a form and forms can be long, confusing, and time-consuming. This could affect the experience of the user and drive him away from the platform. The challenge here is to design a form that would be easy to understand and fill.

User Research

We intereviewed 6 people to understand thier problems.

Key Outcomes from personas

  • Manage my team and distribute tasks
  • Meet other entrepreneurs who believe in the same cause as I do
  • Connect with investors with the same social values as myself
  • Spend less time doing paperwork

Prototype

Designing Forms

We wanted our user to have an decluttered and simple experience while filling the forms. Hence we decided to take some inspiration from Typeforms which has some great form designs. What we Liked was that it was very focused and presented one question at a time which was important for our purpose.

However doing this complicated the navigation, as you now need to navigate to each and every question and we needed a solution for it.
