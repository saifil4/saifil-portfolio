Plato

Wheel for change was an investment platform for Social Entrepreneurs where Potential investors could find and invest in projects. The Client required a digital platform in which Social entrepreneurs could create their profiles for their projects and could be found by investors.

Client

Ashraful Hasan

My Role

Product Designer

Duration

3 months

Challenges

User had to input in a lot of information through a form and forms can be long and boring. This could affect the experience of the user and drive him away from the platform. The challenge here is to design a form which could takes hours to fill(not at the same time).
