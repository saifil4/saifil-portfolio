Plato

Plato is an web app that helps users find learning content and resources relevant to a specific profession. It curates, recommends and provides links to already existing youtube videos based on user preferences.

My Role

Product Designer

Duration

3 months

Responsibilities

User Research
Visual Design
Product Design

The Problem

Everyday thousands videos are uploaded on youtube out of which some are related to eLearning. Youtube might not be an eLearning platform but many people come to yotube to learn skills like programming, design etc. As a matter of fact in 2018 86% people said that they use youtube to learn something in a survey.

However, Youtube is a video sharing platform not a elearning platform and lacks some features of it. Also since the non elearning content on the platform is way more in quantity then elearning content it is more likely to be in suggested videos which has become a major source of youtube views. This leads to challenges especially in the initial stages of youtube career where you need a certain amount of views to get monitized.

The Solution

A platforms which which is primararily for learning content where youtube creators can embed thier vidoes on the platform and engage and have more chances of engaging with thier target audience to achieve more views.

Persona

We interviewed 6 people to Understanding Social Entrepreneurs and thier problems and based on that determine set of features to solve thier problems
